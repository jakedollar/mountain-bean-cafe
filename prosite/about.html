Our Story

Tired of bland coffee on their Colorado hiking trips, our founders out to solve a problem: where could adventurers find a truly great cup? With no local roaster meeting their standards, they opened Mountain Bean Café in 2020 to serve fresh, sustainable roasts to fellow trailblazers.

Our promise is simple: premium coffee that fuels your adventures and a cozy shop that feels like home. From sourcing ethical beans to crafting the perfect pour-over, we’re here to make every sip unforgettable for tourists and locals alike.
